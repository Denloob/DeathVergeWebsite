    info

    First of all, there are only two rules on the server:

    1. No cheat clients.

    2. To have fun!!

    Every player starts with three lives and will lose one of them when he's killed by another player

    You can check how many lives you have left by running: /trigger mylives

    Any group of three players can request to become a team. Team members see each other when invisible, can use their team chat, and most importantly can craft an anchor.

    Your anchor will give your team any effect you want, forever, until destroyed.
